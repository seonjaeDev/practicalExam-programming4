About SHJCS

One of the greatest ambition of all parishes is to have a good parochial school to ensure the christian education of a great portion of the children of the parish.

Mission

Through the ever-compassing love of the Sacred Heart of Jesus, the SHJCS is commited to:

  1. Promote religion as the heart of the curriculum by providing a nurturing Catholic learning environment and fostering Gospel Values and and well-being.
  2. Developing holistic individuals who will become persons of character and competence.
  3. Producing graduates who will become productive and responsible servant leaders.
  4. Promoting love of country and nationalism.
  5. Exercising efficient management and delivery of services.

Vision

To the people called by the Father in Jesus Christ, to become a community of persons with fullness of life, witnessing to the kingdom of God by living the Paschal Mystery with Mary as Companion.

Academic Programs

Sacred Heart of Jesus Catholic School (SHJCS) offers a range of academic programs designed to provide a holistic education to its students. Here are some of the key programs:

Basic Education

  1. Kindergarten: Early childhood education focusing on foundational skills and values.
  2. Elementary: Grades 1 to 6, emphasizing core subjects and character formation.
  3. Junior High School: Grades 7 to 10, preparing students for higher education with a comprehensive curriculum.
  4. Senior High School: Grades 11 and 12, offering various tracks to cater to students' interests and career goals.

Senior High School Tracks

  1. Academic Track:
    • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM): For students interested in science and technology fields.
    • Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM): For those inclined towards business and management careers.
    • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS): For students interested in social sciences, education, and related fields.
    • General Academic Strand (GAS): For students who are undecided on a specific track but want a broad academic foundation.
  2. Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track:
    • Home Economics: Focuses on practical skills for home management and related careers.
    • Information and Communications Technology (ICT): Prepares students for careers in IT and related fields.
  3. Arts and Design Track: For students with a passion for the arts, including visual arts, music, and design.
  4. Sports Track: For students who wish to pursue careers in sports and physical education.

Special Programs

  • International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme: A rigorous pre-university program recognized worldwide for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education.

These programs aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible.
